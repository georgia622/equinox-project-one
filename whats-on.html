What's On at Equinox

Curated Evenings, Seasonal Immersion, Timeless Atmosphere.

At Equinox, each event is crafted to reflect the spirit of the season and the rhythm of the city.
From intimate tasting flights and live music pairings to hands-on cocktail ateliers, our calendar celebrates the art of gathering with intention. We invite you to explore what's ahead—and join us for a moment worth remembering.
Contact us to find out more or fill out our events form to signup and join us.

Midsummer Reverie

Saturday, July 20 | From 7PM
Celebrate the height of summer with Midsummer Reverie, an immersive evening of elegance under the soft glow of candlelight and curated soundscapes. Join us for a sensory journey through the flavours of high summer — from herbaceous spritzes to rare stone fruit infusions.
Tickets: £35 per guest | Includes a welcome cocktail.

August Ember

Saturday, August 24 | From 7PM
As summer begins to soften into its final notes, join us at Equinox for August Ember—an elegant evening of slow cocktails, smouldering flavours, and live, open-air ambience. A Live rooftop saxophone + vinyl DJ set, blending soulful jazz with ambient grooves
Tickets: £40 per guest | Includes welcome drink, access to rooftop event space.

Every Thursday — Tasting Flight Nights

Thurdays | From 7PM - 8:30pm
Join us for an intimate weekly tasting event where our head bartenders and spirit specialists lead you through a curated flight of exceptional pours — from rare single-origin distillates and seasonal infusions to artisanal liqueurs and cask-aged expressions.
Tickets: £38 per guest | Includes all spirits and guided tasting.
Capacity: Limited to 16 guests, booking required.

Monthly — The Equinox Cocktail Atelier

Upcoming dates: June 13th, July 11th | 6:30pm-8pm
A hands-on cocktail-making class in our private lounge. Learn the craft of balance, technique, and seasonal pairing from our master mixologists. Includes guided tastings, a take-home recipe portfolio, and a complimentary cocktail on arrival.
Tickets: £45 per guest | Includes all ingredients, tools, and a welcome drink.
Capacity: 12 guests per class.
