Welcome to Equinox, Where Cocktails Align With the Seasons.

At Equinox, we believe that every cocktail is an experience—one shaped by time, place, and the rhythm of nature. Tucked away in the vibrant city centre of Newcastle upon Tyne, Equinox offers an elegant escape from the ordinary.
Whether you're joining us for a quiet evening cocktail or one of our curated seasonal events, you'll find a sanctuary of refinement just steps from the city's historic streets.

A Journey Through the Seasons

Inspired by the changing seasons, our menu evolves in harmony with the calendar, offering meticulously crafted cocktails that reflect the spirit of each solstice and equinox. From delicate spring botanicals to deep winter aromatics, every drink tells a story of place, time, and intention.

Where exceptional evenings begin with thoughtful detail

At Equinox, service is an art — quietly attentive, never hurried, always personal. From the moment you arrive, our team is dedicated to creating a seamless, sensory experience. Whether guiding you through our seasonal cocktail menu, offering tailored pairing suggestions, or simply anticipating your next need before you voice it, we serve with intention, discretion, and grace.
